Snacks are the best thing in the world.

Why?

Reasons:

  1. Eating every few hours is the key to keeping your blood sugar stable.
  2. Snacks are an opportunity to fuel the body with important and essential nutrients.
  3. A healthy snack actually keeps your weight down.
  4. Hangry… Need I say more?
  5. It’s a stress-free time to take for yourself.

Snack ideas

  • Carrot sticks and cherry tomatoes
  • Cereal bread sandwich with chicken and tomato
  • Banana and peach smoothie
  • Yogurt with berries or granola or nuts
  • Cashews and grapes
